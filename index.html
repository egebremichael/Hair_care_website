Dirt and build-up can clog follicles and even affect shedding and hair growth, so proper cleansing is essential for healthy hair. 


Curly hair is inherently dry, so conditioning is ESSENTIAL every wash day. You should only ever detangle your hair under the shower after you have applied your conditioner, never when it's dry!


Because of its texture, curly hair struggles to maintain moisture and nourishment in between washes, so using a leave-in is key.


For shiny, defined, bouncy curls, you need stylers. There are lots of formats: creams will provide a soft crunch-free hold, foams are great to boost volume and gels are best for high definition.
